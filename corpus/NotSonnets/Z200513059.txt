Read with regard, what here with due regard,
Our second Ciceronian Southwell sent,
By whose persuasive pithy argument,
Ech well disposed eye may be prepared,
Respectiuely their grief for friends decease
To moderate without all vain excess.

Sith then the work is worthy of your view,
Obtract not him which for your good it pend:
Unkind you are if you it reprehend,
That for your profit is presented you,
He pend, I publish this to pleasure all,
Esteeme of both then as we merit shall:
Wey his works Worth, accept of my goodwill,
Else is his labour lost, mine crossed, both to no end:
