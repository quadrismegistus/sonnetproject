You girls whose talk is all of Pop,
you're showing off to us
in your halter-tops and slacks.
Your looks announce with such mild cruelty
that this is all there is
and all there ever was
of happiness in Young America.
'It's the best-in-the-world!' you say
when you stand your boyfriends up
just to be here tonight,
revolving, alone, in the spotlight.
'Try it, you'll Like it,' you say,
'It's the Real Thing.'
When you dance in close
