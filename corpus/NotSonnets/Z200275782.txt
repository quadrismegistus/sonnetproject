Where does that Beutie & that Sweetnes lie
            Whereby
    Thou charmest generous Spirits, who
    With might & main thy business do;
        Thy monstrous business, which
All other Witcheries does far outwitch.

Art Thou not stuffd with Bitterness and Gall?
            Is all
    Thy Trade not full of gnawing Passions,
    Of Discontents, & self-vexations?
        Doth not the boiling heat
Of thy fell Bosome, make thy self its meat?

O costly sin; what thanks to Heavn We owe,
            That Thou
