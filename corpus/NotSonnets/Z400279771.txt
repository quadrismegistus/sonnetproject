By nature we are all defiled with sin,
And with the Leper must cry out, Unclean.
Sin is and impure [?] Issue, none can bring
Purity thence, this a polluting thing;
A sinner's heart's so black, that only hell
Can pattern it, and be it's parallel;
But Grace is a spiritual Lavor, stiled
Regenerations washing; Souls defiled,
Faith & Repentance cleanse; Grace lays the soul
A whitening, searches wounds, and makes them whole.
Grace is of a Celestial nature, though it
Wholly remove not sin, it does subdue it;
Mary's tears as they washed Christs feet, so
