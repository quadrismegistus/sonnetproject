To God, our strength, with joyful voice,
    in triumph let us sing:
To Jacobs God, with echoing noise,
    make we the air to ring.

Take up the Psalmes sweet melody,
    the sounding Timbrell bring:
The pleasant Harp, the Psalterie,
    strain voice, and wind, and string.

Blow up the Trumpet, when the Moon,
    her silver horns renews:
At solemn feast, as to have done,
    on our high day we use.

For this a statute was ordained,
    to Israel foretold:
