St Andrews town may look right gawsy,
Nae Grass will grow upon her causeway,
Nor wa'-flow'rs of a yellow die,
Glour dowy over her Ruins high,
Sin Samy's head weel pang with' lear
Has seen the Alma Mater there:
Regents, my winsome billy boys!
'Bout him you've made and unco noise;
Nae doubt for him your Bells' would clink,
To find him upon Eden's brink,
An' a' things nicely set in order,
Wad kep him on the Fifan border:
I'se warrant now frae France and' Spain,
Baith Cooks and Scullions money ane
