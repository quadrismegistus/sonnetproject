life is no story. Might we recall, then, what it is?

Below, a steamer preserved from the pre-First-War haven

A city gardener drenched rosebud clods with a hose.
To wait there above Leman halted by a layover
dearly companioned and, meeting this other, to let
afternoon's vast cup exhale vaporous fire
was to tap a vintage I had forgotten planting.
