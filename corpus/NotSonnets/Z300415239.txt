Attend unto my tears O Lord, [Side note: 1Kb]
    regard my woeful moan:
And seek to save me by thy word,
    or I am overthrown.

For sin does so oppress my mind,
    that I am damned to hell: [Side note: 1Kb]
Unless by Christ I favour find,
    whose wounds must make me well:

Cure thou my soul so sick with sin
    by merits of thy son:
Mark not the state that I live in,
    but mark what he hath done.

Most perfect he (though I be vild) [Side note: 1Kb]
    to please when I offend:
