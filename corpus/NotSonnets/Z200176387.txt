Oh, de worril is room' en de worril is wide -- 
    Lord! 'member deze chillun in de Morning' -- 
Hit's a mighty long ways up de mountain side,
En day ain't no place fer dem sinners fer ter hide,
En day ain't no place whar sin kin abide,
    W'en de Lord shill come in de Morning'!
        Look up en look aroun',
        Fling the' burden on de groun',
    Hit's a-gittin' mighty close on ter Morning'!
        Smoove away sin's frown -- 
        Retch up en git de crown,
    W'at de Lord will fetch in de Morning'!

De han' er ridem'shun, hit's hilt out ter you -- 
    Lord! member dem sinners in de Morning'!
