Hark! over the lake rings
    My lover's guitar;
Hush my found heart -- Lord!
    He'll wake up Papa!

Nearer! Oh now he'll
    His found suit renew -- 
My hair's all in papers -- 
    Oh! what shall I do?

Hist! to his voice chimes
    The gondolier's oar -- 
Heavens! my Pa wakes!
    No! 'to was but a snore!
