Awf'lest boy in this-here town
Er anywheres is Elmer Brown!
He'll mock you -- yes, and' strangers, too,
An' make a face and' yell at you, -- 
    "Here's the way you look!"

Yes, and' wunst in School one day,
An' Teacher's looking' wite that way,
He helt his slate, and' hide his head,
An' maked a face at her, and' said, -- 
    "Here's the way you look!"

An'-sir! when Rosie Wheeler smile
One morning at him 'crosst the aisle,
He twist his face all up, and' black
His nose wiv ink, and' whisper back, --
