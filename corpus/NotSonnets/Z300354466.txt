I Like spondees. I used them when I adapted a few lines
from the poem Long View for the fourth movement of the
secular cantata number I, This Is Our Time, music by Will- 
liam Schuman.


              We will make our peace   (true peace)
              Our wealth                     (true wealth)
              And our justice                (true justice)


    It was very nice to hear these rhythms sung.
    A poem called Lark (Collected Poems, 1918 -- 1938), was
composed on the spondee plan, but I did not write it with
the idea that it would be set to music or sung. There was a
primitive music in my own head when I wrote it, but when
I finished the poem I closed that off as if to cut connections
with chaos. This poem has been set three times, each time
