The felon tongue of Rupilie,
    that traitor mongrel king,
How Persius hath dressed in kind,
    it is no novel thing.
As common as the carts way that.
    This Persie for the most,
Did make his bode at Ginnia:
    with Rupilie at host.
With gybes, and glickes, and taunting strife
    a brawler sharp and soar
Rash, arrogant, and by use had
    of ribaldry such store:
That from a dosen cackling drabbes,
    the bell he might have boar
