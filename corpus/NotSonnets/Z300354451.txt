How live with grief but to live it through,
See it wear thin and feel it fade.
How slowly sags the day; the few
Arts of relief are ghostly dead.

Ah, joy believer, where are you now?
Your morning whistle, your midnight will,
Foolish sounds and gesture. How
Spectre, explain ... I doubt thee still!

The old say softly, yes.
The old say softly, no.
The young exclaim O
And weep and rebel, so.

And spend themselves until
At last they are the old
