Upon a hill I herd them talking
    big Gog and little Magog:
'I thought by now I should know better.'
    Said little Magog to Gog.

'All I know is that the sun rises
    then sets below the tree.
By now I aught to know why this
    odd thing happens to be.'

Said Gog to little Magog,
    'And why me and you?
And what and where and when and how?
    And who my God is who?'

Said little Magog to Gog
    as they looked down at the sea,
